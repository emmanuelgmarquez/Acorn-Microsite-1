About Acorn Stairlifts

Since our earliest days in 1992, Acorn has strived to set new standards in quality and innovation for the stairlift industry. Our pioneering designs and engineering excellence have led the way and helped Acorn grow to become the world's leading independent company specialising solely in the manufacture and installation of stairlifts.

Our head office is located in Florida with a network of stairlift surveyors and engineers all over the USA. Over 20 years experience and knowledge means that we are America's leading stairlift manufacturer. Acorn remains a down-to-earth business that has never lost sight of the most important asset at its very core — our customers.
